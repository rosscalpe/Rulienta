My story

I entered the technology world driven by a desire to find work that would give me more quality time with my family. I wanted to pursue something I was passionate about while having the flexibility to work from home—something every mother truly needs.

In 2021, I began this career journey that keeps me deeply immersed in technology today. The unique combination of technology, music, and motherhood defines who I am now. This is Rosemary Calderón—Rulienta—a brand representing a curly-haired girl full of life, motivation, and an innovative future ahead.

Balancing my roles as a developer, musician, and mother has taught me the value of adaptability and creative problem-solving. Each aspect of my life enriches the others, creating a unique perspective that I bring to every project and collaboration.
